Our Vision

To be a leading Institution Rotaract club in Uganda, empowering young leaders to create positive change in their communities and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide opportunities for young people to develop leadership skills, engage in community service, and foster international understanding through Rotaract.

    • In collaboration with fellow Rotaract clubs such Rotaract Club of Mulago and the Rotaract Club of MTAC, we launched a powerful Say No to Drugs campaign.
